Features we offer

Real time monitoring of parks and suggestions on visiting the parks, the parameters monitored are listed below

Temperature

The DHT11 temperature sensor is an essential component for our real-time data website, providing accurate and up-to-the-minute temperature information. This cost-effective digital sensor can measure temperatures in the range of 0 to 50 degrees Celsius, delivering precise data that is crucial for various applications.

Humidity

The humidity sensor we employ in our real-time data website is a key component for delivering precise and up-to-the-moment humidity information. Our chosen humidity sensor, often the DHT11, offers reliable measurements of relative humidity in the range of 20% to 80%.

IR sensor

Our website employs Infrared (IR) sensors for real-time motion detection, enhancing security and automation features. These sensors detect changes in the infrared radiation within their field of view, making them highly effective in monitoring movement.

LDR sensor

In parks, our website employs Light-Dependent Resistor (LDR) sensors to provide an enhanced and energy-efficient lighting solution. These sensors are pivotal in automatically turning on park lights as darkness falls, creating a safer and more inviting environment for park-goers.
